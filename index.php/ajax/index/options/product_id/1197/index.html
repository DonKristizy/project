Product Details




From the moment your baby arrives, you realise you want to protect him the best you can. The Graco Coast car seat helps you to keep your baby safe in the car and is classed as a group 1, making it suitable for children weighing between 9 and 18kg. A 5-point safety harness and reinforced side impact protection provides the optimum safety for your child.


5 reclining positions ensure your child is comfortable at all times and the lock-off system ensures your child is positioned correctly. Extra protection is provided by the performance harness, which is doubled with anti-slip material to absorb energy during a crash. As your child grows the back of the Graco Coast seat can be removed and it can be used as a low booster seat.
