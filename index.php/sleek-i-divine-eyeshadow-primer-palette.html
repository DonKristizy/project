Product Info

A palette of 12 eye primers designed to be used with all Sleek MakeUP eye shadows. You can find your base colour from a selection 12 shades which can be used to enhance and intensify your chosen eyeshadow.

  • It is a light cream formula that intensifies shades giving the truest colour pay off, providing a good base and prolonging the wear of your eye makeup look

     

Weight

12X1.1g/12x0.03oz

Storage Info

Store in a cool dry place

Not tested on animals

Suitable for vegans and vegetarians

Ingredients

How to

Choose a similar primer shade to your chosen Sleek MakeUP eyeshadow and use your fingertips to blend evenly across the eye socket before applying eyeshadow.
